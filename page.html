Punit Renjen (born 1961)[1][2] is an Indian-American businessman who has been chief executive officer of the multinational professional services firm Deloitte since June 1, 2 015. Previously, Renjen was chairman and CEO of Deloitte Consulting LLP, and later, held the role of chairman of Deloitte LLP (United States) from 2011 to 2015. Renjen was raised in Rohtak, North India in the state of Haryana. After earning a Master of Management from Oregon's Willamette University, he was hired by Touche Ross, which merged into Deloitte in 1989. He has worked at Deloitte and lived in the US ever since.
